Storage management

This component is for the management of the data stored within this browser. In the future you will be able to come here to bulk export/import your data to a '.json' file for backup and transfer. At this current point in time the only function of this component is to see how much browser storage this web app has left for its use.

None of your measured data, or your model data is stored on a remote server. Your measurements and models are only sent to the server in the form of x and y coordinates for parameterisation, and in the form of widths, lengths, and measured factors for modelling. The server does not save a copy to disk of any of that data.

In the future it is envisaged that an option will be provided for you to easily share your model with the community under the AGPLv3+ license should you so choose, but this will not be automatic. It will be opt-in only.
